About Us: Olive Wood

OLIVAWOOD is a Factory using Olive Wood to produce items for kitchen & table, furniture, decor and gift items. We are based in Sousse city in the center of Tunisia, where the olive trees are abundant. We are using more than 7600 M2 for production. We are in treatment and supply of olive wood since 1988. Our experience and know- how are source of our success. Today we are able to produce, supply and satisfy large customer orders, fast and in high quality. We produce upon customer request, design and models.
